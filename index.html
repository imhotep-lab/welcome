Presentation

Imhotep_Lab is a IT laboratory devoted to computer science, electronics and automatism.

Imhotep_Lab according the tutelage of his Project Manager wants topromote the infatuation to be interested in information and communications technology as well as nanotechnology. The backwardness that Africa undergoes in this field, new pillars of the current development of the countries, is alarmist. This justifies the initiative to create within the African youth interest in this pivotal field of development of modernity and promoters of a new economic vision in Africa.

Personalized trainings with very high added value

Each session is limited to 20 participants per classroom, which enables us to personalize your formation according to your needs and of your expectations. Whatever your field in IT, IMHOTEP_LAB uses very high pedagogic methods teaching to boost efficiently your knowledges and abilities in programming and make you become a genius programmer .

Promoting GNU/Linux operating systems and other Unix open-sources operating systems

Our laboratory shares GNU/Linux's philosophy. So, IMHOTEP_LAB promotes GNU/Linux operating systems, such as Debian, Ubuntu , and so on .

If you use GNU/Linux, you are free. If you promote GNU/Linux Operating Systems and share the GNU philosophy, you are the best and you contribute to make the world a better place.
